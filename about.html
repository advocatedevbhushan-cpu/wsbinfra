About WSB Infra & Crafts

Building the future with integrity, innovation, and excellence since 2013.

Engineering Tomorrow's Infrastructure

WSB Infra & Crafts is a diversified engineering, procurement, and construction company committed to reshaping India's infrastructure landscape. Since our inception in 2013, we have grown into a trusted partner for industrial, commercial, and government sectors.

Our core expertise spans Pre-Engineered Buildings (PEB), government infrastructure projects, and comprehensive engineering services — from concept design to turnkey execution. We combine advanced technology with on-ground craftsmanship to deliver structures that are safe, sustainable, and cost-efficient.

With a team of 50+ experienced engineers, project managers, and fabrication specialists, we serve clients across industrial, institutional, and public sectors with tailored solutions.
