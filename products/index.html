Our Products

High-quality construction materials and structural solutions — manufactured with precision, delivered with reliability.

Product Range

From structural steel systems to sustainable building materials — we manufacture everything under one roof.

Pre-Engineered Buildings

Design, fabrication, and erection of PEB structures — warehouses, factories, stadiums, aircraft hangars, and cold storage with clear spans up to 90 metres using high-tensile steel conforming to IS 2062 / ASTM 572.

Explore PEB

WPC — Wood Plastic Composite

Eco-friendly Wood Plastic Composite boards, decking, cladding, and fencing. Made from recycled wood fibre and thermoplastics — rot-proof, weather-resistant, zero-maintenance, and available in multiple finishes.

Explore WPC

Fly Ash Bricks

Eco-friendly, high-compressive-strength fly ash bricks manufactured from thermal power plant waste. Uniform size, low water absorption, excellent thermal insulation — a sustainable alternative to traditional clay bricks.

Explore Bricks

Steel Structures

Custom-engineered structural steel frameworks for industrial, commercial, and infrastructure projects — portal frames, multi-storey frames, trusses, bridges, and transmission towers fabricated as per IS, AISC, and AWS standards.

Explore Steel
