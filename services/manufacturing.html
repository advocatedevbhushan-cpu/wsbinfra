Steel Manufacturing

State-of-the-art fabrication facility for structural steel, PEB components, and custom metal works — precision engineering from concept to delivery.

Our Manufacturing Facility

WSB Infra & Crafts operates a 50,000+ sq. ft. state-of-the-art steel fabrication facility equipped with the latest CNC machinery, automated welding stations, and a dedicated quality control laboratory. Our plant is capable of producing over 1,500 metric tons of fabricated steel per month.

We specialize in the fabrication of pre-engineered building components (primary & secondary members), structural steel for industrial sheds, bridges, transmission towers, and custom architectural metalwork. Every component is fabricated as per approved shop drawings and undergoes rigorous quality inspection before dispatch.

Our facility is ISO 9001:2015 certified and follows lean manufacturing principles to ensure on-time delivery with zero-defect quality.

Machinery & Equipment

Advanced machinery for precision steel fabrication and finishing.

CNC Plasma Cutting

Hypertherm HPR260XD plasma cutting system with automated CNC control for precise cutting of plates up to 50mm thickness with minimal kerf loss.

CNC Beam Drilling & Sawing

Ficep beam processing line for automatic drilling, sawing, and marking of H-beams and channels — increasing productivity and accuracy.

Automated Welding Stations

Submerged Arc Welding (SAW) and MIG/MAG welding stations with robotic manipulators for consistent, high-strength weld joints meeting AWS D1.1 standards.

Hydraulic Press Brakes

CNC-controlled press brakes for precise bending of plates and sections for custom profiles, brackets, and architectural components.

Sandblasting & Painting

Automated shot blasting and painting line with surface preparation to SA 2.5 standard, followed by epoxy and polyurethane coating application as per project specifications.

Overhead Cranes

Multiple EOT cranes with capacities from 5T to 50T for safe material handling and movement within the fabrication shop.

Quality Control Process

Every component undergoes multi-stage quality inspection before dispatch.

01

Raw Material Inspection

Incoming steel from SAIL/JSW/Tata is verified against mill test certificates. Chemical composition and mechanical properties are tested in our lab before acceptance.

02

In-Process Inspection

Dimension checks, weld inspection (visual & ultrasonic), and fit-up verification at each fabrication stage by qualified QC inspectors.

03

Non-Destructive Testing

Ultrasonic testing (UT) of critical welds, magnetic particle inspection (MPI) for surface defects, and dye penetrant testing (DPT) for non-porous materials.

04

Coating Inspection

Surface profile measurement, ambient condition monitoring, dry film thickness (DFT) measurement, and adhesion testing on all coated surfaces.

05

Final Assembly Check

Trial assembly of critical connections, bolt hole alignment verification, and camber/deflection checks as per approved drawings.

06

Dispatch & Documentation

Material marking, packing as per standard, and complete documentation including I-certs, MTC, weld maps, and inspection reports for each shipment.
