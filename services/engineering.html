Engineering Services

Comprehensive structural engineering, BIM modeling, project management, and technical consulting for infrastructure projects.

Our Engineering Expertise

From concept design to construction supervision — our team delivers engineering excellence at every stage.

01

Structural Design & Analysis

Advanced structural engineering using STAAD Pro, Tekla Structures, and ETABS. We perform detailed load analysis (dead, live, wind, seismic, crane) and deliver optimized, code-compliant designs that balance safety with cost-efficiency.

02

BIM & 3D Modeling

Full Building Information Modeling (BIM) services for clash detection, quantity take-offs, construction sequencing, and stakeholder coordination. Our Tekla models ensure fabrication-ready shop drawings with zero errors.

03

Feasibility Studies

Technical and financial feasibility assessments including site analysis, geotechnical investigation, load calculations, cost-benefit analysis, and risk assessment for informed decision-making before project commitment.

04

Project Management

End-to-end project management from concept to handover. Services include planning & scheduling (MS Project/Primavera), resource allocation, vendor coordination, quality assurance (QA/QC), and safety management.

05

Structural Audits & Inspection

Comprehensive structural health assessments for existing buildings and infrastructure. Services include non-destructive testing (NDT), ultrasonic weld inspection, load testing, corrosion assessment, and detailed condition reports.

06

Technical Consulting

Expert advisory services for material selection, construction methodology, value engineering, foundation design, and regulatory compliance. We help clients optimize project costs without compromising quality or safety.

Software & Tools

We leverage the latest engineering software for precision and efficiency.

STAAD Pro

Structural analysis and design for steel and concrete structures — fully compliant with IS, AISC, and Eurocode standards.

Tekla Structures

Advanced BIM and detailing software for steel structures — generating fabrication-ready shop drawings and NC files for CNC machines.

ETABS

Building design and analysis for multi-story structures with advanced seismic and wind load analysis capabilities.

MS Project / Primavera

Project planning and scheduling tools for critical path analysis, resource leveling, and progress tracking.

AutoCAD

Detailed 2D drafting for architectural, structural, and MEP layouts with precision dimensioning and layer management.

Navisworks

Clash detection, 4D construction sequencing, and project review for coordinated and conflict-free project delivery.
