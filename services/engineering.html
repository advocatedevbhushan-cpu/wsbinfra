Engineering Services

Comprehensive structural engineering, BIM modeling, project management, and technical consulting for infrastructure projects of all scales.

Our Engineering Expertise

From concept design to construction supervision — our team delivers engineering excellence at every stage of your project.

01

Structural Design & Analysis

Advanced structural engineering using STAAD Pro, Tekla Structures, and ETABS. We perform detailed load analysis (dead, live, wind, seismic, crane) and deliver optimised, code-compliant designs that balance safety with cost-efficiency. All designs comply with IS 456, IS 800, IS 1893, and relevant international codes.

02

BIM & 3D Modelling

Full Building Information Modelling (BIM) services for clash detection, quantity take-offs, construction sequencing, and multi-stakeholder coordination. Our Tekla models ensure fabrication-ready shop drawings with zero errors, reducing rework by up to 30%.

03

Feasibility Studies

Technical and financial feasibility assessments including site analysis, geotechnical investigation, load calculations, cost-benefit analysis, and risk assessment. We help clients make informed decisions before committing capital to a project.

04

Project Management

End-to-end project management from concept to handover. Services include planning & scheduling (MS Project / Primavera P6), resource allocation, vendor coordination, QA/QC, safety management, and progress reporting.

05

Structural Audits & Inspection

Comprehensive structural health assessments for existing buildings and infrastructure. Services include non-destructive testing (NDT), ultrasonic weld inspection, load testing, corrosion assessment, and detailed condition reports with repair recommendations.

06

Technical Consulting

Expert advisory for material selection, construction methodology, value engineering, foundation design, and regulatory compliance. We help clients optimise project costs without compromising quality or safety.

Software & Tools

We leverage the latest engineering software for precision, efficiency, and coordination.

STAAD Pro

Structural analysis and design for steel and concrete structures — compliant with IS, AISC, British, and Eurocode standards.

Tekla Structures

Advanced BIM and detailing platform that generates fabrication-ready shop drawings and NC files directly from the 3D model.

ETABS

Building design and analysis for multi-storey structures with advanced seismic and wind load analysis capabilities.

MS Project / Primavera

Project planning, scheduling, critical path analysis, resource levelling, and earned value management for projects of any scale.

AutoCAD

Detailed 2D drafting for architectural, structural, and MEP layouts with precision dimensioning and layer-based organisation.

Navisworks

Clash detection, 4D construction sequencing, and project review for fully coordinated, conflict-free project delivery.

Our Engineering Process

A systematic approach to delivering engineering excellence.

Concept & Brief

Understand project requirements, site conditions, client expectations, and regulatory constraints to define the engineering scope.

Analysis & Design

Structural modelling, load analysis, member design, and optimisation using industry-standard software and design codes.

Detailing & Documentation

Detailed shop drawings, bar bending schedules, bill of materials, and technical specifications for construction and procurement.

Review & Approval

Internal QA/QC review, third-party peer review if required, and client approval before release for construction or fabrication.
