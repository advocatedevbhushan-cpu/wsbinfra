Government Infrastructure

Trusted EPC partner for public sector infrastructure — delivering quality, transparency, and timely execution.

EPC Contractor for Public Sector

WSB Infra & Crafts is a registered and empanelled EPC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction) contractor for government infrastructure projects. We deliver end-to-end solutions for central government agencies, state public works departments, urban local bodies, and public sector undertakings.

Our government project portfolio covers institutional buildings, rural infrastructure, bridges, electrification, and community facilities. Every project is executed with strict adherence to tender specifications, quality standards, and statutory compliance.

We are fully compliant with all government procurement guidelines, including GST, PAN, EPF, ESIC, and labour law registrations — ensuring smooth bid submission and post-award execution.

Government Project Capabilities

Comprehensive EPC services for public sector infrastructure needs.

Institutional Buildings

Government office complexes, schools, colleges, hospitals, and community centers — designed and built to government specifications with all amenities.

Roads & Bridges

Rural and urban road construction, bridge approaches, culverts, and flyovers with proper drainage, signage, and safety features as per IRC/MoRTH standards.

Rural Electrification

Design and installation of distribution networks, transformer stations, pole erection, and last-mile connectivity under government electrification programs.

Water Supply & Sanitation

Overhead tanks, piped water supply networks, sewerage systems, and community sanitation blocks for urban and rural local bodies.

Health & Education Infrastructure

Primary health centers, school buildings, anganwadi centers, and skill development facilities with barrier-free design and modern amenities.

PPP Projects

Experience in Public-Private Partnership project execution, including BOT and annuity models for infrastructure development.
