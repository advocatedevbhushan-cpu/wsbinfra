Pre-Engineered Buildings

Design, fabrication, and turnkey erection of world-class PEB structures — faster, stronger, and more cost-effective than conventional construction.

What Are Pre-Engineered Buildings?

Pre-Engineered Buildings (PEB) are steel structures designed, fabricated, and assembled using factory-controlled processes. Every component is precision-engineered off-site and bolted together on location — eliminating traditional construction inefficiencies.

PEB technology delivers 30–50% faster construction and 20–30% cost savings compared to traditional methods. Structural members are designed using advanced 3D modelling software (STAAD Pro, Tekla), manufactured in controlled factory conditions with CNC precision, and erected by trained teams with minimal on-site cutting or welding.

Our PEB solutions are engineered to IS 2062 and ASTM A36 standards, with clear spans up to 90 metres and custom bay spacing for any application. The factory-controlled environment ensures consistent weld quality, precise dimensions, and superior surface finishing with minimal on-site work.

All our PEB projects are backed by a comprehensive warranty on materials, workmanship, and structural integrity — giving you complete peace of mind.

Benefits of PEB

Why industry leaders choose pre-engineered buildings over conventional construction.

30–50% Faster Construction

Parallel site preparation and factory fabrication reduce project timelines dramatically. A 50,000 sq.ft. warehouse can be completed in 60–90 days — not 6 months.

20–30% Cost Savings

Optimised steel usage, reduced labour costs, lighter foundations, and faster occupancy deliver significant overall project savings.

Clear Spans Up to 90m

Column-free interiors for maximum layout flexibility — ideal for warehouses, hangars, sports arenas, and manufacturing plants.

Superior Quality Control

Factory fabrication ensures consistent weld quality, dimensional accuracy within ±1 mm, and full compliance with IS/ASTM standards.

100% Sustainable & Green

Steel is fully recyclable. Factory processes minimise material waste by 15–20%. Energy-efficient insulation options reduce operational carbon footprint.

Easily Expandable

Modular design allows future expansion in length, width, or height with minimal disruption to existing operations.

Types of PEB Structures

Custom PEB solutions for diverse applications across industries.

Industrial

Warehouses & Logistics Hubs

Large clear-span warehouses with dock levellers, mezzanine floors, and integrated material handling systems. Ideal for 3PL, e-commerce, and manufacturing storage.

Industrial

Factories & Manufacturing Plants

Custom-designed production facilities with crane beams (up to 20T), overhead utilities, and partitioned sections for offices and worker amenities.

Institutional

Sports Arenas & Stadiums

Column-free clear-span roofs for indoor stadiums, multipurpose halls, and sports complexes with seating galleries and acoustic treatment.

Industrial

Cold Storage & Food Processing

Insulated PEB structures with PUF panels, temperature-controlled environments, and hygienic finishing for food and pharmaceutical storage.

Infrastructure

Aircraft Hangars

Wide clear-span hangars with bi-fold doors, integrated maintenance pits, and compliance with airport authority standards.

Commercial

Showrooms & Retail Complexes

Architecturally finished PEB structures with glazing, mezzanines, and modern facades for car showrooms, retail outlets, and exhibition centres.

Our PEB Process

A streamlined 4-stage approach from concept to commissioning.

Design & Engineering

3D modelling, structural analysis, shop drawings, and MEP coordination using STAAD Pro, Tekla, and Navisworks.

Fabrication

Transportation
