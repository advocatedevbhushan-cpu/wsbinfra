Pre-Engineered Buildings

PEB Solutions

Pre-Engineered Buildings (PEB) represent the pinnacle of modern construction efficiency. Our PEB solutions are designed using advanced 3D modeling software, manufactured in factory-controlled conditions, and erected with precision — delivering structures that are 30-50% faster to build and 20-30% more cost-effective than conventional construction.

Speed

Parallel site work and factory fabrication cut project timelines by up to 50%.

Cost Efficiency

Optimized steel usage reduces material and labor costs by 20-30%.

Clear Spans

Column-free spaces up to 90m for unobstructed layouts.

Quality Controlled

Factory fabrication ensures consistent quality, dimensional accuracy, and IS compliance.

Sustainable

100% recyclable steel, minimal waste, and energy-efficient building envelopes.

Expandable

Modular design allows easy future expansion with minimal operational disruption.

Engineering Services

Comprehensive Engineering Solutions

Structural Design & Analysis

Advanced structural engineering using STAAD Pro, Tekla Structures, and ETABS for optimized, code-compliant designs that ensure safety and cost-efficiency.

BIM & 3D Modeling

Building Information Modeling (BIM) for clash detection, quantity take-offs, and construction sequencing — reducing errors and improving project coordination.

Feasibility Studies

Technical and financial feasibility assessments including site analysis, soil investigation, load calculations, and cost-benefit analysis for informed decision-making.

Project Management

End-to-end project management from concept to handover — including planning, scheduling, resource allocation, quality assurance, and safety management.

Structural Audits & Inspection

Comprehensive structural health assessments, non-destructive testing (NDT), load testing, and detailed condition reports for existing structures.

Technical Consulting

Expert technical advisory for material selection, construction methodology, value engineering, and regulatory compliance throughout your project lifecycle.

Our Facility

Manufacturing & Fabrication Plant

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with the latest CNC machinery, automated welding systems, and a dedicated quality control laboratory. With a monthly fabrication capacity of 25 metric tons, we serve projects ranging from small industrial sheds to large multi-span PEB complexes.

CNC Cutting & Profiling

Automated plasma and oxy-fuel cutting systems for precise plate profiling with tolerances within ±1mm.

Submerged Arc Welding

Automated SAW machines for high-quality, defect-free longitudinal and fillet welds on primary members.

CNC Drilling & Punching

Precision drilling and punching for bolted connections ensuring perfect fit during on-site erection.

Blasting & Painting

7-stage surface preparation and painting line — shot blasting to Sa 2.5, followed by zinc-rich epoxy primer and polyurethane topcoat.

In-House QC Laboratory

Dedicated testing lab for tensile strength, bend tests, ultrasonic weld inspection, paint thickness, and hardness verification.

Logistics & Dispatch

Efficient material handling and dispatch systems ensuring timely delivery to project sites across the region.
