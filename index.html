Our Journey

From a small fabrication workshop to a multi-specialty infrastructure company.

2013

Foundation

WSB Infra & Crafts established as a steel fabrication workshop in Hyderabad with a 5,000 sq.ft. facility and a team of 8 skilled fabricators.

2016

Expansion into PEB

Entered the pre-engineered building market. Completed first 50,000 sq.ft. warehouse project. Facility expanded to 20,000 sq.ft. with new CNC equipment.

2019

Government EPC Entry

Registered with CPWD, NHAI, and State PWD. Won first government infrastructure project — a 40-classroom school complex. Team grew to 35+.

2021

ISO Certification & Expansion

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification. Facility expanded to 50,000 sq.ft. Added robotic welding and automated painting line. Reached 150+ project milestone.

2024+

Pan-India Presence

Serving clients across 8 states with a team of 50+ professionals. Diversified into engineering consulting and structural audits.
